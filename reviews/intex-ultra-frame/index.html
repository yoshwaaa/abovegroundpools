Intex Ultra Frame Above Ground Pool Review

The Ultra Frame is not only one of Intex’s most popular products, it’s one of the highest selling above ground pools on the market, and with good reason - it’s an Intex product, which means you have a perfect balance of quality, durability, and affordability, but it also has some extra bonuses and features that beat out the rest of the Intex lineup. Mix that with the pool’s optional square or round shapes and a truly outstanding mix of build quality and portability, and the Ultra Frame’s popularity is immediately justified. If you’re shopping for a great above ground pool, the Ultra Frame should be at the top of your list.

But is this pool the right size for your yard and family? And does it have enough features to justify the higher price point? Read our review to find out.

Pool Sizes

Like many other above ground pools, Intex gives you several options for size. This is great for families that want a quality pool, with everything you need included, but want to tailor the size of their Ultra Frame for to their individual needs. The size choices for the Ultra Frame include:

  • Rectangular - 24 feet by 12 feet (52 inches high)
  • Rectangular - 32 feet by 16 feet (52 inches high)
  • Round - 16 feet (48 inches high)
  • Round - 18 feet (52 inches high)

These are perfect sizes. The 32-foot square pool is absolutely massive, and large families, or those that really value having massive pool parties, will love this pool. For those that want something smaller, or don’t quite have the yard space for a 32-foot pool, the 24-foot square option is still more than enough room for parties or families with several children.

However, if you prefer a more traditional rounded above ground pool, both the 16 and 18-foot pools offer good size. This is enough space for most uses, and the larger square options are only necessary for those that want to have a massive pool system.

Build Quality

It’s an Intex product, so this means the quality and assurance of a good company is there. That being said, the Ultra Frame is probably Intex’s most sturdy product, with PVC sidewalls, a steel frame, and a sand filter pump. All the extras here are high quality, and the core materials are sure to last many years without issues.

However, as with several other products in the Intex line, the included ladder isn’t exactly top of the line. It’s more than enough to get you started, and we have no doubt it will last the lifetime of the pool in most cases. However, if you’re interested in the Ultra Frame, and don’t mind spending a little extra, we’d recommend purchasing a higher quality ladder. Don’t let this put you off though, as everything included with the pool is all you need to get started, and absolutely safe enough for family use.

If you’d like to learn more about pool ladders, and what to look for if you’re interested in purchasing a separate ladder from your pool, check out our Above Ground Pool Accessories Guide.

What’s Included

The Intex Ultra Frame has everything you need to get started, and honestly, all you really need to enjoy your pool for years to come. Here’s what you get in the box:

  • A steel pool frame
  • Ground cloth that will line the bottom of your pool
  • A stand-up ladder
  • A pool liner
  • A debris cover
  • A sand filter pump (rated from 110 - 120v)

In addition to the standard equipment included above, the Ultra Frame also features some small improvements and premium features that set them apart from other pool types. The frame snaps together, using Intex’s “Easy Lock System”. This eliminates the need for other tools, and helps speed up the setup time for the Ultra Frame. This is a great feature, and the system is very sturdy. Some buyers may not trust using a frame that doesn’t rely on screws and bolts to hold everything together, but most reviews highlight the Easy Lock System as a great feature.

Installation and Maintenance

The Ultra Frame products take more time than other Intex pools, due to their size and the more extensive features, but you still won’t see very long setup times. Most users say they were able to have the pool set up in around an hour, not counting how long it takes to fill the pool with water. While not quite on par with Intex’s Easy Set pool system, the Ultra Frame still boasts a fantastic setup time, and since it doesn’t need any additional tools, snapping the frame together is relatively simple as well.

However, the most overlooked part of an above ground pool’s setup is also the most important part - leveling the ground. You need to make sure the spot where the pool is going has been measured out, and there are no bumps or dips in the ground. This creates pressure in the bottom of your pool liner, and over time, this can weaken and destroy the liner. By making sure the ground is flat before you start setting up your pool, you can drastically increase the life of your liner, and your pool.

For more tips on how to set up an above ground pool, read our Above Ground Pool 101 Guide.

Buying Options

In most cases, we recommend purchasing your pool online, through a large retailer like Amazon or Home Depot. They have warehouses all over the world, which means sending a pool to your house is usually pretty quick, and offer the reliability that many smaller shops simply can’t.

However, if you’ve got a local pool shop that you trust, this is always a great option. Having someone close to you that will be there to answer questions or help you in case you need assistance setting up your pool is extremely valuable.

Conclusion

The Ultra Frame is Intex’s flagship product, and it shows. It has a long-lasting frame and liner, great size choices, and maintains the company’s knack for creating safe, easy to set up and maintenance products. It’s the perfect pool for most families. If you’ve been shopping around a bit, and still haven’t found the best pool for you and your loved ones, we’d bet the Ultra Frame will be the above ground pool for you.
