Intex Easy Set Pool Set Review

Intex seems to be the king of simple, straightforward above ground pool designs, and nowhere is this more evident than the Easy Set Pool. With a setup time of about 15 minutes, and all the extras you need to create a portable and safe pool for your family, the Easy Set is a perfectly suitable pool for many buyers. But does this mean it’s the best pool for you and your loved ones? Read on for our thoughts.

Build Quality and Warranty

The pool measures 10ft in diameter, and the outer material is mostly made of vinyl. There is also an inflatable soft edge that covers the top of the pool wall, which means you can rest easy with younger children playing in the pool - the materials do a great job of protecting kids (or energetic young adults) from hurting themselves on the edges, which is a common complaint with larger pool systems that use stainless steel frames. The pool isn’t very tall, measuring at only 30 inches in height, but this is great for buyers with small children, or those that only plan to use the pool for their immediate family/smaller pool parties.

With the focus on the pool’s portability and value, some users have complained the the Intex Easy Set is a bit too flimsy compared to many other options. We won’t argue here. The pool isn’t made for massive parties or years of abuse - it’s a simple, affordable setup that should fit many families wanting a good pool at a great price. However, if you are looking for something bigger, or something with more durability, we’d highly recommend another Intex product, the Ultra Frame.

We should also mention that seasonal storage with this pool is a very good option. This means you pack up the pool during the winter months, and bring it out again in the summer. With such a short setup time, the Easy Set is the best pool we’ve reviewed for seasonal storage.

What’s Included

For the price, the Easy Set provides tons of value, but you will likely need to buy a pool pump, unless you purchase the option that includes a filter/pump combination. You will also receive a basic ladder, and an instructional DVD that gives you a quick rundown on how to set up the pool and general maintenance advice.

One special thing to note about the Easy Set is the drain plug - it’s garden hose compatible, which means you hook up a hose and drain the water as far away from the pool as you’d like. This is a fantastic benefit, and can be very beneficial for families that want to store the pool quickly in the winter months.

Installation and Maintenance

This one of the easiest pools on the market to setup. Intex says the pool can be ready for water in about 10 minutes, and while we’d recommend taking your time, especially if you’ve never set up an above ground pool before, the setup for the Easy Set is as straightforward as it gets.

As with any other above ground pool, the ground where you’d like to place the Easy Set needs to be completely flat before you begin setting up the pool. If there are any dips or rises in the ground, this creates pressure points, which will begin wearing down the liner over time. Even for families that do seasonal storage, built up pressure in the floor of your pool, especially over years of use, can drastically reduce the amount of life in your liner. Properly flattening the ground before hand is the most important, and most overlooked, step in setting up the perfect above ground pool for your family.

If you purchase an Intex pool with the included water pump, be warned that the pump is not as rugged as some of the more expensive options. Several buyers have reported that the pump gives out quicker than they were expecting, but this isn’t surprising given the pools affordability. If you would like to purchase a more expensive and reliable pump, we’d recommend buying the Easy Set without the included pump, and purchasing a separate device on your own.

Read our Above Ground Pool Pump Guide for more information on pump types and tips on purchasing the right pump for your pool.

Buying Options

Thanks to Intex’s popularity, you’ve got plenty of options when it comes to purchasing an Easy Set. For this type of pool we’d highly recommend online sellers like Amazon or The Home Depot, although you can also purchase a pool through Intex directly.

We highly recommend purchasing a pool like this online from a trusted dealer for the best price/reliability, but of course, if you have a pool dealer near you that you trust, they will likely have several Intex options in stock.

Conclusion

If you and your family want a compact, easy to setup pool, it’s tough to beat the Intex Easy Set. They provide an outstanding product at a great price.

For buyers that want something bigger, or a pool that they can easily expand, the Easy Set may not be the best option, in that case, we’d recommend going for something like the Intex Metal Frame, which has some more space and comes with more options.

In the end though, the Intex Easy Set is exactly what the makers intended it to be - a portable, safe pool that will provide you and your family years of enjoyment. If you have younger children, or would like a pool that you can store during the winter months, or even if you just want an affordable starter pool for your family to see how much use it gets, the Intex Easy Set may just be the best above ground pool for you.
